Who I am

Army Veteran to Software Engineer

At the age of 18, I joined the Army as a 35T - Military Intelligence Systems Maintainer. As I always grew up with a love for computers and video games, I was excited to start a new journey where I was going to be working on Systems all over the world while serving my country.

While serving, I made education a priority and started working on my bachelor's degree in Information Technology with a concentration on Web Development. This concentration opened the world of Software Engineering for me and ever since, I was hooked.

Today, I am getting ready for my transition away from the military to pursue a career in tech full-time where I wish to work on the latest technologies available. I am currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology.
